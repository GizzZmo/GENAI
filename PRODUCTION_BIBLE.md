## 1. WORLD STRUCTURE — THE THREE LAYERS

### Layer 1 — The Surface World

The public sees a city where music, tech, and culture blend seamlessly.
Studios, labels, and creators appear free.

But the surface is curated, monitored, and shaped by corporate influence.

### Layer 2 — The Hidden Network

A decentralized underground of open‑source creators, rogue AI architects, and system‑level thinkers.
They communicate through code, sound, and encrypted commits.

### Layer 3 — The Lab

Jon‑Arve's studio is the most advanced node in this network.
It is disguised as a music production space but functions as:

- An AI research facility
- A surveillance analysis hub
- A DSP development center
- A distributed signal‑routing cluster
- A sanctuary for open tools

**This is the heart of the story.**

## 2. TECHNOLOGY BIBLE — HOW EACH SYSTEM BEHAVES

*This section is for UI/UX teams, VFX teams, and gameplay engineers.*

### Face‑Recon (The Eye)

- Displays 1, 4, 8, 16, 32, or 64 live feeds
- Bounding boxes, confidence scores, camera IDs
- AutoSec overlays appear when threats are detected
- Feeds can glitch when the corp attempts intrusion
- Used for tension, foreshadowing, and plot reveals

### DSP4Guitar (The Voice)

- Real DSP code visible in panels and cutscenes
- Plugin UI must match real JUCE‑style interfaces
- Corp backdoor is hidden in a legitimate function
- Ghost Coder's patch removes it
- Used for emotional beats and plot turning points

### AI_shell (The Tongue)

- Terminal‑driven AI interface
- Natural language → system actions
- Used for hacking, decoding, and communication
- Ghost Coder speaks through it
- UI must feel raw, fast, and dangerous

### DJ Python Mixer (The Pulse)

- Controls all connected machines
- Commands look like real Python CLI tools
- Routing visualizations resemble audio patchbays
- Used for action sequences and cluster‑wide events

### Master Prompt Editor (The Mind)

- Vertical monitor
- Metadata graphs, agent nodes, prompt trees
- Used for philosophical moments and AI design scenes
- Represents the "soul" of the lab

## 3. CHARACTER BIBLE — EXPANDED PROFILES

*This section is for writers, animators, and casting.*

### Jon‑Arve Constantine

- Speaks rarely, but with precision
- Moves like someone who thinks in systems
- Never raises his voice
- Emotional core: **responsibility vs. isolation**

### The Hacker

- Talks fast, types faster
- Humor as a shield
- Emotional core: **chaos vs. purpose**

### The DJ

- Calm, rhythmic, almost meditative
- Emotional core: **harmony vs. avoidance**

### Ghost Coder

- Voice is glitched, fragmented
- Emotional core: **guilt vs. courage**

### The Envoy

- Always smiling
- Emotional core: **loyalty vs. conscience**
